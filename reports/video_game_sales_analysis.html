1. Business Context¶

In this project, I act as a data science consultant for a video game company that seeks to gain better insights into the global video game market. The video game company is interested in learning which video game genres, platforms, publishers, and regions achieve the highest sales. This information can be used to inform business decisions regarding video game development, platforms to use for development, and regional marketing and launch planning.

The dataset used for this analysis contains historical video game sales data, including game titles, platforms, years of release, genres, publishers, and sales for North America, Europe, Japan, and worldwide. By analyzing this data set, the video game company can gain better insights into the market and use this knowledge to inform its business decisions.

The purpose of this notebook is to create an explanatory data analysis pipeline that analyzes and answers business questions based on the data set using Python and Pandas.

Final Discussion & Recommendations¶

In this notebook, I used the historical video game sales data to answer important business questions for a video game company. From this analysis, it is evident that certain genres and platforms perform better than others in terms of video game sales. Also, it is evident that different geographical areas, such as North America, Europe, and Japan, play different roles in video game sales.

In addition, this analysis proves that publisher strength and platform/genre pairs are key drivers for video game success. Trend analysis for different years gives a broader view of video game trends, and analysis of top-selling video games provides examples of successful video game market trends.

From a business perspective, the main recommendation that can be made is that a business should consider future game development in genres and platforms that have historically performed well while also being aware of regional differences in terms of consumer preferences. However, it should be noted that the results should be taken into account while keeping in mind that the data set being analyzed is historical and does not necessarily reflect the current state of the gaming industry.

In my opinion, The main limitation of this kind of analysis is that it only takes into account sales and does not have any other parameters included in the data set.
